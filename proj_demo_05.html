Project Budgeting - Monte Carlo Simulation (Python)

项目预算 - 蒙特卡罗模拟分析

The Monte Carlo simulation is a statistics method used to determine an outcome in the presence of uncertainty/external influences. For instance, it is widely used in option pricing to determine the price of an option at expiration. In the prospective project, I demonstrate how we can use the Monte Carlo method to estimate the COGS/gross profit across 1000 IT implementation projects.

本项目使用蒙特卡罗模拟方法来分析1000个IT实施项目在期末所产生的成本/毛利，用于公司预算编制或项目立项时可行性的评估。
